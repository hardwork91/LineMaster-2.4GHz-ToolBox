What is LineMaster 2.4GHz?

LineMaster 2.4GHz is a control system specifically designed for control line flying, combining the ergonomics of classic handles with modern 2.4GHz radio technology. More than just a transmitter and receiver, it is a modular and evolving platform capable of remotely controlling throttle, flaps, retractable landing gear and many other functions.
Aimed at pilots who enjoy sport flying as well as Scale and Carrier categories, LineMaster is ready to use straight out of the box. The goal is to expand the possibilities of the hobbyâ€”making it more accessible, safe and versatileâ€”while staying true to its roots and building a bridge between radio control and control-line flying.
The story behind
It started after reading an article in Model Aviation magazine, where many C/L pilots relied on modified transmitters and improvised DIY solutions. As a software developer and aeromodeling enthusiast, Carlos saw a clear opportunity: create something built from the ground up specifically for control line, ready to use without extra work.
With the support of his long-time friend Eduardo de las Casas and the experience of Bob Whitney, the project evolved through countless test flights, 3D-printed prototypes and hours of tuning. An early prototype even appeared in the December 2024 issue of Model Aviation, giving the project a strong push forward.
Today, LineMaster 2.4GHz is moving from prototype to a robust, ready-to-use platform that we plan to keep improving togetherâ€”with a shared goal: enjoy and grow this amazing hobby.
