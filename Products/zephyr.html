Single-channel handle for pilots who just want to enjoy flying without extra complications. Lightweight, comfortable and straight to the point. Modern technology in a classic control-line format, ready to use right out of the box.

Key Features

Proportional Throttle

Single-channel proportional throttle control for simple and reliable flying.

OLED Display

128×64 monochrome OLED with 5-way directional switch for intuitive navigation.

Built-in Timer

Integrated countdown timer up to 15 minutes with configurable sound alerts.

Unlimited Model Profiles

Store unlimited configurations with 14-character names. Customizable home screen via SD card.

Advanced Settings

Endpoint adjustment, exponential curves, channel inversion and per-model failsafe settings.
