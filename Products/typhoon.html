Handle · 5 Channels

Typhoon

5-channel handle designed with Carrier and Scale pilots in mind. Delivers the power and versatility of a commercial transmitter in a compact, ergonomic, and lightweight format that stays true to the spirit of control-line flying.

Buy on Etsy User Manual

Key Features

Proportional Throttle

Smooth throttle control with engine cut-off capability for safe landings.

4 Auxiliary Channels

Control flaps, retracts, lights, hooks and more with configurable auxiliary channels.

5 Movement Generators

Random motion generators for scale-realistic engine effects during flight.

OLED Display

128×64 monochrome OLED with intuitive menu navigation via 5-way directional switch.

Built-in Timer

Integrated countdown timer up to 15 minutes with audible alerts and haptic feedback.

Unlimited Model Profiles

Store and switch between unlimited model configurations (14-character names). Customizable home screen via SD card.

Multi-Language

Multi-language interface support. Firmware updates via SD card keep your handle current.

170+ lb Pull Tested

Slide & Lock mechanism tested to over 170 lbs. Ready to fly out of the box.
