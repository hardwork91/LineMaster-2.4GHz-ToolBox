Scheme Generator

Visual design tool for creating and exporting color schemes for control-line model aircraft. Design your livery, preview it in real time and share it with the community.

About this Project

The Scheme Generator is a browser-based tool that lets you paint and design color schemes directly on a model silhouette. Choose colors, patterns and markings, then export your design as an image or share a link.

This project is currently under active development. Features, interface and capabilities may change significantly as it evolves.

Planned Features

Model Silhouettes

Paint directly onto accurate top-view and side-view model outlines.

Color Picker

Full color palette with support for custom hex values and saved swatches.

Export & Share

Download your scheme as a PNG image or share it via a short link.

Pattern Library

Choose from built-in geometric patterns and classic control-line liveries.
